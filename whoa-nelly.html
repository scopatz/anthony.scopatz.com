Whoa Nelly

Sat 13 May 2006

So I went out hitting the bars & clubs in Santa Barbara last night with Shawn.

Man was that fun. It turns out I really like the two drinks I ordered: A White Russian and a shot of Don Julio tequilla with salt and a lime.

By far the best music I heard was when I put a buck into the Juke Box. My Playlist?

ZZ Top - Cheap Sunglasses
James Brown - Papa’s got a Brand New Bag
Frank Sinatra - Forget Domanni (sp?)

By Far the Sinatra was the most danceable music I heard for the entire night. This makes me really look forward to spending Christmas in Las Vegas. My family and the Rowes are planning on meeting up there. I wanna go get drunk in a lounge casino bar with my cousins and listen to Jazz malet ensemble or Sinatra Cover band. That would be bliss.

In other news, last night I ran into my friends Tanya and Irene. That first time was fine. The second time reminded me of Neil Young’s “The Needle and The Damage Done.” Here he says that “Every Junkie is like a Setting Sun.” Tanya was about as wasted as I have ever seen a friend and Irene was more on top of things but that was because she had just thrown up every drink she had that night. So one was super drunk and one was just sad. I could type the whole saga, but really, why bother?

Cheers!
